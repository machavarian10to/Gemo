## The problem Gemo solves

You open the fridge. Nothing inspires you. You scroll through your phone trying to figure out what to cook. Sound familiar?

Gemo is built around that exact problem. It gives you food recommendations based on what's trending, what your community is cooking, and even what ingredients you already have at home. No more blank stares at the fridge.

---

## Core features

### 🥘 What to eat — right now

**Pantry** is one of Gemo's most useful features. You tell it what ingredients you have, and it generates recipe ideas you can actually make. No missing ingredients, no wasted food, no decision fatigue.

- Open Pantry, add your ingredients
- Get AI-generated recipe ideas instantly
- Save the ones you like to your recipe collection
- Come back to them whenever you need inspiration

**Daily Meal Plan** takes it further — AI plans your full day (breakfast, lunch, dinner) based on your dietary preferences, so you're not making that "what to eat" decision three times a day.

---

### 🍽️ Gems — recipes from real people

Beyond AI, Gemo has a whole community sharing what they cook. Posts are called **Gems** and come in three types:

- **Plates** — photo recipes with descriptions, ingredients, and hashtags
- **Bites** — short video recipes
- **Polls** — ask the community things like "pasta or risotto tonight?"

Your feed shows gems from people you follow and communities you're in, so recommendations get better the more you use it.

---

### 🔍 Discover & Search

Not sure where to start? Browse **Discover** for trending recipes and featured creators, or search by hashtag to find gems around a specific dish, cuisine, or ingredient. The more the community posts, the better your recommendations get.

---

### 👥 Communities & Pages

Join communities around cuisines or dietary styles you care about — like "Healthy Meal Prep" or "Italian Cuisine Lovers". Every community has its own feed of gems, so it's a focused stream of relevant food ideas.

**Pages** let restaurants, chefs, and food brands share their recipes too.

---

### 📖 Stories

Quick food moments that disappear after 24 hours — dishes you tried, meal prep progress, food polls. Think of it as the casual side of Gemo.
